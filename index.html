“We select advanced compounds and technological solutions to achieve the highest performance.”

Hyalolite products are manufactured under GMP cosmetics ISO (ISO 27716) and have CE certified quality system to guarantee the maximum safety and quality (ISO 13485 – ISO 9001).
Hyalolite products contain sonicated Hyaluronic Acid, an exclusive patent procedure created by LEM Compounding Research which allow to fragment the molecular weight of raw materials.
We sonicate in our laboratory the Hyaluronic Acid that is fractionated in low, medium and high molecular weight with an ultrasound sonicator.
Thanks to our exclusive protocol of sonication to produce a high percentage of low molecular weight HA (University of Varese ITALY).
For our formulations we use innovative functional ingredients that is responsive to skin necessities. We combine specific ingredients such as Mimicking growth factors, Hyaluronic Acid, Ceramides, Vitamins and PDRN. We select advanced compounds and textures that ensure the best stability and bioavailability. Thanks to new technological innovations and continuous research. is a scientifically developed skin care solution for both personal and professional products.
